Brett Weir

Founder and CEO

Brett originally developed the LameStation as his senior design project at UC Irvine, where he studied Electrical Engineering.

Brett believes that the key to success is passion and genuine excitement about whatever you do. That's why he quit his job as a software engineer to start LameStation LLC, where he hopes to bring the joy of building your own game console to as many people as he can.

When Brett isn't developing game consoles, you can probably find him writing music. He is a published songwriter and has been songwriting since middle school.
